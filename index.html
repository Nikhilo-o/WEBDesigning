About KIET

KIET Group of Institutions, established in 1998, is a premier educational institute in India. Offering a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines such as Engineering, Management, and Applied Sciences, KIET is dedicated to creating global leaders in education, innovation, and industry.

Located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, KIET has a state-of-the-art campus, a world-class faculty, and a vast network of alumni excelling across industries worldwide.
